About Clear Skies

A practice built for survivors, open to everyone

Clear Skies Counseling began with a specific mission: guiding people through the deep and often isolating journey of recovery from narcissistic abuse. That work remains our heart.

As we grew, something beautiful happened — people reached out with other needs, and the safe space we built for survivors became a place of healing for anyone navigating anxiety, depression, grief, family dynamics, or life transitions.

"Your story matters, and we are honored to be part of your healing journey."

Our Approach

Evidence-based, always personalized

You'll help create a therapy plan that fits your needs, drawing on proven modalities.

Talk Therapy FOUNDATION

Time and space to share what you're carrying, make sense of it, and find healthy ways to cope — whether it's stress, anxiety, relationships, or past trauma.

EMDR TRAUMA

Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing helps your brain process painful memories so they lose their grip — like your body healing a cut, naturally.

CBT PRACTICAL

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy connects thoughts, feelings, and actions. Work on specific goals and practice new patterns so you feel more in control.

DBT BALANCE

Dialectical Behavior Therapy blends mindfulness with skills for managing big emotions, staying calm under stress, and communicating your needs.
